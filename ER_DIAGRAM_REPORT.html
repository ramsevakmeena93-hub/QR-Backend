1. Overview

The system uses MongoDB as its database with 8 collections. Each collection represents a core entity of the attendance management system. The database name is mits-attendance.

Collections: User  |  Subject  |  Class  |  QRSession  |  Attendance  |  ClassMaterial  |  StudentMarks  |  Feedback

5. Key Business Rules

▶ Maximum 3 QR codes per day per class — tracked by generatedDate field
▶ No duplicate attendance per student per date — enforced by unique index on (student + class + date)
▶ QR auto-expires after 8 seconds — TTL index on expiresAt
▶ Auto grade calculation: ≥90%→A+ | ≥80%→A | ≥70%→B+ | ≥60%→B | ≥50%→C | ≥40%→D | <40%→F
▶ Passwords are bcrypt hashed with salt rounds = 10
▶ Anonymous feedback option hides student identity from teacher
▶ Manual vs QR attendance distinguished by markedBy field
